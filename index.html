About AI Learning Gym

AI Learning Gym is a free collection of browser-based tools built for developers, writers, students, and anyone who needs quick, reliable utilities without signing up for an account or worrying about their data being uploaded somewhere.

Every tool on this site runs entirely in your browser. When you paste text into the JSON formatter or generate a password, that data never leaves your device — there's no server processing it, no database storing it. It's just your browser doing the work locally. That means the tools are fast, private, and available even on a slow connection.

What's available

The tools are organized into four main categories:

  • Text and AI tools — including an AI content detector, word counter, case converter, diff checker, and font generator. Useful for writers, editors, and content teams.
  • Developer tools — JSON formatter, Base64 encoder/decoder, password generator, QR code generator, and Markdown editor. The tools developers actually reach for throughout the day.
  • Math and finance calculators — loan amortization, tip splitter, unit converter, and age calculator. Straightforward calculators that give you the numbers you need without distractions.
  • Health and wellness tools — BMR and TDEE calculator, BMI calculator, sleep cycle planner, and Pomodoro timer. Practical tools for tracking health metrics and managing focus.

How to use the tools

Click any tool card above to open it. Each tool has a simple input area at the top — type or paste your content, adjust any settings, and the result appears immediately. There's no loading screen, no wait time, and nothing to install. When you're done, copy or download your result and you're finished.

Why we built this

Most online tools either require you to create an account, show you ads before you can use them, or quietly send your input to a server. We wanted something simpler: a clean set of tools that just work, respect your privacy, and don't get in the way. AI Learning Gym is a single place where you can handle the small tasks that come up constantly — without switching between a dozen different sites.

Frequently asked questions

Are the tools free?
Yes, everything on the site is free with no account required.

Do the tools work on mobile?
Yes. The site works on phones and tablets, though some tools like the diff checker or JSON formatter are easier to use on a larger screen.

Is my data private?
Yes. All tools process your input locally in your browser. Nothing you type or paste is sent to any server. When you close the page, your data is gone.

Why is it called AI Learning Gym?
The site started as a collection of AI-related tools — particularly around understanding and detecting AI-generated content. It's grown into a broader toolkit since then, but the name stuck.
